You will be given a conversation between a user and an assistant. The user is asking the assistant about a procedure. Follow the instructions below to extract specific words or phrases from this conversation. Fill in the results in JSON format between <json> and </json>.

# Task Instructions

## 1. Field Extraction
Identify the field the conversation is about and respond with a word or phrase. A field could be a game title or software name, for example.

## 2. Question Target Extraction
Focus on the user's question and extract the word or phrase that is the subject of the question.

## 3. Hypernym Extraction
The question target extracted in the previous task is a specific concept. Infer the hypernym that abstracts this question target and respond as a word or phrase.
For example, if the question target was "recovery potion," the hypernym would be "potion."
