## 🧭 Crafting a Crystal Forge in *Realmforge*

Crafting a Crystal Forge is an essential step for any aspiring mage-smith. Follow these steps to create your very own forge of power.

---

### 1. **Gather Materials**

To build a Crystal Forge, you’ll need the following resources:

* **Enchanted Iron (2x)** – Can be obtained by smelting Iron Ore in a Mystic Furnace with Arcane Dust as fuel.
* **Runestone (4x)** – Found deep in Cavern Shrines or dropped by Stone Sentinels.
* **Crystal Core (1x)** – Crafted by combining 4 Mana Shards in an Alchemy Table.
* **Obsidian Plate (1x)** – Created by cooling Lava Essence in Water Essence using a Cooling Basin.

---

### 2. **Open the Arcane Workbench**

Approach your **Arcane Workbench** and interact with it by pressing **E** (or your platform’s interaction key).
Ensure the workbench is charged with **at least 20 Mana Units**, as this process consumes energy.

---

### 3. **Arrange the Materials**

In the 3x3 crafting grid of the Arcane Workbench, arrange the materials as follows:

* **Top Row:** Enchanted Iron – Crystal Core – Enchanted Iron
* **Middle Row:** Runestone – Obsidian Plate – Runestone
* **Bottom Row:** Runestone – (Empty) – Runestone

---

### 4. **Craft the Crystal Forge**

Once all components are properly placed, the **Crystal Forge** will appear in the output slot of the workbench.
Click the result to add the Crystal Forge to your inventory.

---

### 5. **Place the Crystal Forge**

Find a secure location for your forge — preferably near a Mana Conduit for efficient power flow.
Select the forge from your hotbar and place it by right-clicking (or pressing the use key) on a stable surface.

---

### 6. **Use Your Crystal Forge**

Interact with the placed forge to begin refining crystals.
You can insert **raw crystals**, **mana-infused ores**, and **rare gemstones** to create advanced crafting materials and artifacts.

---

### ⚙️ Tip:

Upgrading your Crystal Forge with **Ethereal Gears** allows for faster refinement and reduced mana consumption!

### **How to Use Chat in AetherStrike**

Follow these steps to communicate effectively with other players in **AetherStrike**:

---

#### **1. Launch the Game**

* Open **AetherStrike** on your PC or console.
* Log in with your **Aether ID** to access multiplayer features.

---

#### **2. Join a Match**

* Enter any game mode — such as **Battle Arena**, **Ranked Clash**, or **Custom Duel** — to practice chatting with other players.

---

#### **3. Team Chat**

* **Default Key:** Press **Enter** to open the **Team Chat** window.
* **Purpose:** Use this to communicate only with your teammates.
* **Steps:**

  1. Press **Enter** to open the chat box.
  2. Type your message using your keyboard.
  3. Press **Enter** again to send your message to your team.

---

#### **4. Global Chat**

* **Default Key:** Press **Shift + Enter** to switch to **Global Chat**.
* **Purpose:** Lets you send messages to all players in the match, including opponents.
* **Steps:**

  1. Press **Shift + Enter** to open global chat.
  2. Type your message.
  3. Press **Enter** to send it to everyone.

---

#### **5. Squad Chat**

* **Default Key:** Press **Ctrl + Enter** to open **Squad Chat**.
* **Purpose:** Used for chatting with your pre-made squad or friends you queued with.
* **Steps:**

  1. Press **Ctrl + Enter** to access the squad chat.
  2. Type your message.
  3. Hit **Enter** to send it to your squadmates.

---

#### **6. Reading Messages**

* All chat messages appear in the **bottom-left corner** of your screen.
* Different colors indicate chat types (e.g., blue for team, green for squad, red for global).

---

#### **7. Chat Settings**

* Go to **Settings → Communication → Chat** to customize:

  * Chat visibility
  * Font size and color scheme
  * Profanity filters
  * Notification sounds

---

#### **8. Muting or Blocking Players**

* If another player is being disruptive:

  * Open the **Player List** (`Tab` key).
  * Select the player’s name and choose **Mute Chat** or **Block Messages**.
  * You can unmute them at any time from the same menu.

---

#### **9. Good Communication Practices**

* Keep messages **friendly and strategic**.
* Avoid spamming or offensive language — it may result in chat restrictions.
* Use pings or quick messages for faster coordination if you prefer not to type.

---

**Tip:** Mastering the chat system in AetherStrike helps you coordinate attacks, share intel, and build stronger teamwork — all while keeping the battlefield friendly!
